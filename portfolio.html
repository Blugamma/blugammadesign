    Brand Design Projects

    Gammatrek - Technology Youtube Channel

    This is a design that I created for a youtube channel that did unboxings and reviews of different types of technologies. I kept it simple using a colourful circuitboard as a background and using bold and easy to read typography that makes it visually pleasing for the user to read. I also added some imagery that is related to the topic and lined them up as font underline.

    OCSapps - App Review Youtube Channel

    This design has a logo and banner that could be used in either youtube, twitter, and facebook. These graphics have a minimalist design that keeps the design simple , clean, and easy to understand. I used a slim sans-seif font that was clean. I used multiple different mobile devices that the user maybe using to review apps on to keep the design relative to the purpose.

    Harvey & Martin - Local Organic Hair Salon

    This brand design has a lovely vintage feel using dots and accents to make it suit the design the client requested. The logo is a golden silhouette with flowing hair which uses negative space for the facial features and the positive space to display the hair more boldly making it show that it is a hair salon at a quick glance, I used a stylish vintage typography that makes it suit the design perfectly. This is a brand design that I implimented into the website that I designed for this local business.
